I am Zhiyu Lu, a Ph.D. candidate in Public Finance at the Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE).

My research interests lie at the intersection of Public Finance, Political Economy, and Computational Social Science. I am particularly passionate about applying computational linguistics and machine learning methods (e.g., LLM text analysis, text mining) to analyze government policies, institutional dynamics, and their economic impacts[cite: 19, 26, 53].

My current work focuses on fiscal resilience [cite: 11, 13], digital governance , and enterprise innovation , often leveraging large-scale text data from government procurement contracts [cite: 19, 20], corporate annual reports , and historical archives.
