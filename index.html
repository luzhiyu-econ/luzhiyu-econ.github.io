I am Zhiyu Lu, a Ph.D. candidate in Finance at the Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE). My research interests lie at the intersection of political economy, development economics, and public finance.

I am particularly interested in applying methods from computational linguistics and machine learning (e.g., text analysis, LLMs) to analyze policy texts, institutional dynamics, and their impact on economic outcomes. My current work explores policy diffusion and institutional analysis within the Chinese context.
